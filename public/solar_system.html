Scientific Importance:

The Solar System is our celestial home. It has provided us with everything we know about planetary structures, atmosphere retention, habitability, and life itself. By examining our neighbor planets (from roasting-hot Mercury to icy Neptune), astronomers calibrate exoplanet models to find potentially habitable worlds around distant stars.
