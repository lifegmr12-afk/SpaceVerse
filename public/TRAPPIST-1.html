Scientific Importance:

TRAPPIST-1 houses the largest chain of terrestrial, Earth-sized planets ever discovered in a single exoplanetary system. Because the host star is a dim red dwarf, the planets orbit extremely close to it. The system is a prime target for JWST to search for atmospheric water, biomarkers, and habitable environments.
